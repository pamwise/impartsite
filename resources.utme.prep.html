Best Resources for UTME Preparation

Preparing for the UTME can be challenging, but having the right resources makes a significant difference. Here are some of the best materials and tools to help you ace your exam:

1. Recommended Textbooks

Ensure you use textbooks approved by the Joint Admissions and Matriculation Board (JAMB). Some recommended ones include:

  • "New School Physics" by M.W. Anyakoha
  • "Chemistry for Senior Secondary Schools" by Osei Yaw Ababio
  • "Essential Biology" by M.C. Michael
  • "Longe and Macaulay Mathematics"
  • "Invisible Teacher" by Dele Ashade

2. Past Question Papers

Practicing past UTME questions is one of the most effective ways to prepare. These papers familiarize you with the exam format and highlight commonly tested topics.

3. Online Resources

The internet offers a wealth of materials for UTME preparation. Some valuable online platforms include:

  • MySchool.ng
  • Pass.ng
  • Official JAMB Website

4. Mobile Apps

Several apps are designed specifically for UTME practice. These apps provide questions, answers, and explanations:

  • "JAMB CBT Practice App"
  • "Pass.ng App"
  • "TestDriller"

5. Study Groups

Joining or forming a study group with friends or classmates can help you cover more topics, share ideas, and stay motivated.

6. Video/ Online Tutorials

Platforms like YouTube host numerous tutorial videos on key UTME topics. Channels such as "Khan Academy" and Nigerian-focused educational channels are highly recommended or online tutorial platforms like IMPART with their expert tutors will be highly recommended!

7. Personal Notes

While studying, create concise notes highlighting key points. These notes will be invaluable during your final revision.
